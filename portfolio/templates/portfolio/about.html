Hey there! I am Anirban. I am a Data Scientist.

I have a Masters in Physics, and a deep passion for Mathematics, Science and Technology. I created this blog as a platform to share my love of learning.

Since my career switch from research to industry as a Business Analyst, I have gained experience in planning, designing and improving business systems focused on Customer Engagement and Relationship Management, Agent Recruitment and Sales Force Training, and Channel management in the Banking, Financial Services and Insurance (BFSI) sector.

I recently started my journey in the field of Artificial Intelligence. Aside from a full-time job as a BA, I am currently pursuing two courses: Post Graduate Programme in AI ML from Great Learning and University of Texus, Austin, and IBM Applied AI Professional Certificate. So far, I have learned a few applications of ML in Prediction and Classification problems, and I have also built a Conversational AI Chatbot using IBM Watson!

I am really fascinated with Artificial Intelligence and its business applications, and I can't wait to see where this journey of discovery takes me.
